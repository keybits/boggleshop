About Us

The name “Boggle” has by now become familiar to many of Ireland’s festival organisers and corporate event organisers as well on a smaller scale to parents looking for a children’s entertainer to remove the monumental stress of a kids party.

Our unique interactive circus skills workshops, balloon modellers and expert face painters have appeared at the following events to name but a few:

  • Dublin Temple Bar ESB Summer programme
  • The Rose of Trallee
  • Waterford Spraoi
  • Killarney Summerfest Teddy Bear’s Picnic
  • Fingal County Council Summer Programmes
  • Supervalu Promotions
