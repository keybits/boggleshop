Face Painting

Boggle’s hand picked face painters are renowned for more than your bog-standard faces.

Our faces spring alive:

  • fearsome tigers
  • magical fairies
  • exotic butterflies

Transformations that leave children delighted and parents grabbing for their cameras.

More than just a painter

We also believe that a face painter should be more than just an artist, but a professional entertainer themselves; dressed the part and able to keep the masses amused.

Plus we believe in:

  • professionalism
  • using the best quality paints
  • ad putting hygiene to the fore.

Logistics

The face painters are often booked at the same time as the circus skills workshop. They need to be static and preferably out of the wind and adverse weather conditions. Mind you they’re a gung-ho lot and have been known to persevere in sub-zero conditions.

Charges

Workshops are usually in the region of 2½ to 3 hours. This is open to negotiation, but we do stress that good face painters are worth paying for. They transform your event and get through crowds an awful lot quicker than their less experienced counterparts. Phone us to discuss a price for your event.
