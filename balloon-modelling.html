Balloon Modelling

We also provide for your function expert balloon modellers capable of putting that balloon model dog on a lead and that balloon model sword in it’s own holster.

They come in clowny attire and are more than capable of entertaining the punters as well as being speedy enough to keep the queues at bay.

Balloon modelling is another colourful attraction at any event and leaves many happy kids and adults alike taking their sculptures home.

Logistics

Often the balloon modellers work side-by-side with the circus workshop as a colourful extra but equally they are ideally suited for roaming around the festival / event wherever extra sparkle is required.

Charges

We can offer workshops for around 2½ to 3 hours - call ust to negotiate a price that suits you. The price includes the cost of one modeller plus all equipment. At very large events, two modellers may be required. Long distances incur some travelling costs.
