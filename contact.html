We'll be delighted to tailor a package to suit your needs. As a guide here are examples of the kind of packages we frequently offer:

Kid’s Parties

Boggle clowns are a fun and entertaining bunch with the definite aim of creating a great birthday atmosphere.

We make sure that each and every child is sorted with a host of balloon model hats and sculptures. Every face is beautifully painted.

Over and above this, no party is complete without plenty of silly fancy dress or the circus equipment coming out for a go!

Time and costs

We usually feel that 2 to 2½ hours is the ideal time. The price depends on travelling times, but contact us to negotiate the final package.

The Whole Package

Daunted or just too busy to organise the family entertainment at your event?

We at Boggle are great believers that happy children = happy parents and a really good overall atmosphere.

Our usual package would include:

  • The Circus Skills Workshop
  • One balloon modeller
  • Two face painters

A world of possibilities

We also have access many other performers and shows suitable for family days including:

  • a fancy dress Bedouin tent
  • belly dancers
  • puppet shows
  • and circus acts.
