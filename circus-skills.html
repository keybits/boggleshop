Circus Skills Workshop

There are 100’s of performers out there who can ‘wow’ the socks off audiences young and old alike but we offer a ‘wow’ element that’s a bit different altogether!

We offer the chnace for folks of every age to (toddler to grandparent that is!) to come away a bit of a circus performer themselves. In short interactive hands-on entertainment at your event.

We arrive at events of every type – festivals, corporate events, school fetes, children’s parties – with a cache of circus equipment:

  • balls
  • clubs
  • diablos
  • spinning plates
  • stilts
  • and even unicycles!

Add to this, two expert, friendly circus instructors who can guide anyone who comes along in how to use and master these props.

Everone is catered for

The workshop can cater fo huge amounts of passing punters and everyone from the youngest child to grannies in wheelchairs can grasp something. Organisers are always delighted that we cater superbly with that awkward group ‘the teenagers’ and we are responsible for a good few lads unicycling away in the afternoon.

Furthermore, the Boggle Circus Skills Workshop offers 3 to 3½ hours of solid entertainment, pure colour and a spectacle tha will add a real sparkle to your event.

Logistics

We fit practically anywhere, although a large grassy area is perfect. A hall with relatively high ceilings is also great. We are not scared of the odd drop of rain (or in fact the frequent deluge)! Irish festival goers are a hardy bunch!

Charges

We can offer workshops of 2½ to 3 hours (solidly) which comprise all equipment plus two instructors. Long distances incur some travelling expenses. Smaller functions and parties only require one instructor and shorter hours, so call us to negotiate a price.
